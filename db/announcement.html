Version 2.7.21 released, with updated Gen 7 mechanics!

Version 2.7.21 further expands on previous updates, fixing additional bugs introduced when Generation 7 was added. The most notable fixes include: fixing the learnsets of Alolan formes, improving Hidden Power selection for Generation 7, and multiple battle mechanics.

The Windows, Android and Webclients have all been updated to v2.7.21, with Mac being updated to v2.7.20 at this time. For more information on the latest version of PO, click on the "Latest News" link below.
